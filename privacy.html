Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Overview

AIModelCalc ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at aimodelcalc.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Short version: Your calculator inputs are never sent to us. Everything you type stays in your browser and disappears when you close the tab. We only collect standard analytics and advertising data, described in full below.

Information We Do Not Collect

All three AIModelCalc tools run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your token counts, request volumes, and cost estimates are never transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not store any calculator inputs or results.
  • We do not require an account, email address, or any personal information to use the site.

Information We Do Collect

Like most websites, we automatically collect some basic information when you visit:

  • Log data — your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time on page. Collected automatically by our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages).
  • Analytics data — we may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns. This data is anonymized and not personally identifiable.
  • Cookies — see the Cookies & Advertising section below.

Cookies & Advertising

AIModelCalc is supported by display advertising. We use Google AdSense to show ads on this site. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more information on how Google uses data, visit How Google uses data from sites that use our services.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense — advertising: Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — analytics: Google Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Pages — hosting: Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography loaded from Google's servers: Google Privacy Policy

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are in the European Economic Area or California, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Because we collect minimal personal data, there is very little to act on — but contact us at the email below with any requests.

You can also opt out of interest-based advertising via the Digital Advertising Alliance or Your Online Choices (EU).

Children's Privacy

AIModelCalc is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at: hello@aimodelcalc.com
