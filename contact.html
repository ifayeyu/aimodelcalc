Get in Touch

Pricing corrections get a fast response. Everything else gets a genuine one.

Report a Pricing Error

This is the most important thing you can contact us about. AI API pricing changes constantly and errors creep in. If you spot a stale rate, a wrong per-million figure, or a model that's been deprecated, let us know and we'll fix it fast — usually within 24 hours.

When you email, please include a link to the provider's official pricing page showing the correct rate. That lets us verify and update without delay.

Email for pricing corrections and general questions:

hello@aimodelcalc.com

Pricing errors: typically fixed within 24 hours. General questions: 2–3 business days.

Other Reasons to Reach Out

Feature requests. If there's a model, provider, or calculation type you'd like to see added, send it over. We can't promise to build everything, but we read all suggestions and they influence what gets prioritized.

Content feedback. If something in our guides is wrong, outdated, or unclear — we want to know. The goal is accuracy, not volume, so corrections are genuinely welcome.

Press and media. If you're writing about AI pricing or API costs and want background information, reach out. We're happy to provide context or data.

What We Don't Do

We don't accept guest posts, sponsored content, link insertions, or paid mentions of any kind. We also don't respond to SEO outreach, link exchange requests, or partnership pitches from AI providers. If that's why you're writing, please don't.
