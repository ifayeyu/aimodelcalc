About AIModelCalc

A free tool for developers and businesses to compare AI API costs and estimate real-world spending across GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, and more.

What Is AIModelCalc?

AIModelCalc is a free, browser-based AI model cost calculator and API pricing comparison tool. It was built to help developers, product teams, and businesses understand what they'll actually spend when building on top of AI APIs — before they commit to a model, an architecture, or a pricing plan.

The AI API landscape has expanded dramatically. Where there were once two or three credible options, there are now dozens of models from multiple providers, each with different pricing tiers, context window limits, speed characteristics, and quality profiles. Choosing the right model for a production workload without modeling the cost is a costly mistake. AIModelCalc makes it easy to compare models side by side and project monthly costs at any request volume.

Free and private: AIModelCalc runs entirely in your browser. Your token counts, usage estimates, and cost projections are calculated locally and never stored. No account, no subscription, no data collection.

How AIModelCalc Works

AI APIs charge per token, where a token is roughly 0.75 words or 4 characters of text. Every API request consists of input tokens (your prompt, system instructions, and conversation history) and output tokens (the model's response). Input and output tokens are priced separately — output tokens are typically 3–5× more expensive than input tokens because generating text requires more computation than reading it.

AIModelCalc lets you specify:

  • The model you want to evaluate
  • Your average input token count per request
  • Your average output token count per request
  • Your projected number of requests per month

The calculator then computes your estimated monthly cost, cost per request, and cost per 1,000 requests — and shows you how those numbers compare across every model in our database. Prices are updated regularly to reflect current published rates from each provider.

A Note on Accuracy

AIModelCalc's calculations are based on publicly published pricing from each AI provider. Actual costs may differ due to volume discounts, enterprise agreements, promotional pricing, changes in provider pricing, or differences in how your application uses context windows. We recommend using AIModelCalc's projections as estimates for planning purposes, and verifying final pricing directly with each provider before committing to production infrastructure decisions.

The AI model landscape is evolving rapidly. New models are released frequently, pricing changes on short notice, and new pricing mechanisms (like prompt caching, batch processing discounts, and tiered volume pricing) are regularly introduced. We work to keep AIModelCalc current, but always cross-reference with the provider's official pricing page for the most accurate figures.
